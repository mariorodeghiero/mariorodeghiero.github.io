About Me

Let me introduce myself.

After a solid career of 10 years in the telecommunications area, I decided that after the birth of my son, it would be time to change the area and look for new challenges. Since June 2017, I dedicate my free time to study development, contribute to Open Source projects and improve my English, in the future (beginning of 2019) seek an opportunity as a developer. Currently my studies are focused on Front-end, I have as main programming language Javascript but I also have knowledge in Python. I keep my personal codes and projects on Github and I have also developed my blog to put my learning into practice by publishing articles on development. I am also the creator of "Vue Theme", theme with more than 15 thousand downloads for editor Visual Studio Code.
